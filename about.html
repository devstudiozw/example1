Our History

A Foundation Built on Merit

Founded with a vision to revolutionize primary education in Chivhu, Alpha Junior School has grown into a beacon of hope and excellence. We started with a handful of students and a dream to create a godly environment where merit and integrity are the cornerstones of success.

Today, we host hundreds of students across ECD and Lower Primary grades, providing them with the tools they need to become future leaders of Zimbabwe.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, godly, and nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, critical thinking, and character development in every child from ECD to Primary levels.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Zimbabwe, recognized for producing confident, competent, and ethical leaders who positively impact society.

Message from the Headteacher

"At Alpha Junior, we don't just teach subjects; we mold lives. Our commitment is to ensure that every child walks out of these gates with the confidence to face the world and the faith to move mountains."

We invite you to be part of our growing family. Together, we can build a future that is bright, secure, and full of possibilities for our children.

Mrs. Alpha Chidhumo

Headmaster & Founder
