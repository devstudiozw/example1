Who We Are

About Alpha Junior School

Established to nurture young minds in Chivhu — building literacy, numeracy, and character from ECD through Grade 4.

Our History

A Foundation Built on Quality & Care

Founded with a vision to revolutionize primary education in Chivhu, Alpha Junior School has grown into a beacon of hope and excellence for local families. We started with a passionate team and a dream to create a nurturing environment where every child's potential is recognized and developed.

Today, we host learners across ECD and primary grades, providing them with the academic foundations, social skills, and character development tools they need to become the future leaders of Zimbabwe.

Our Approach

Educational Philosophy

Child-Centered Learning

We recognize that every child learns differently. Our teachers tailor lessons to match each child's unique strengths, interests, and learning pace.

Literacy & Numeracy First

Strong reading, writing, and mathematical foundations are the cornerstone of our curriculum — from ECD through Grade 4.

Character Development

Beyond academics, we instill core values of integrity, discipline, and community spirit to shape well-rounded individuals.

Parental Partnership

We actively encourage parental involvement, believing that the partnership between home and school is key to every child's success.

Future-Ready Skills

Digital literacy, critical thinking, and teamwork are woven into every subject, preparing learners for the demands of the modern world.
