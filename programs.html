Our Curriculum

Academic Excellence for Every Age

Preschool Learning

ECD A & B Foundation

Our Early Childhood Development (ECD) program is designed to provide a smooth transition from home to school. We focus on play-led discovery, sensory learning, and the development of core social skills.

  • Introduction to phonics and numbers
  • Creative arts and music appreciation
  • Motor skills development through play
Primary Education

Grade 1 to Grade 4

Moving into primary school, our curriculum becomes more structured, following the Zimbabwe National Education Standards while incorporating modern global teaching methods.

Grade 1-3

Focus on Literacy, Numeracy, Science, and Heritage Studies.

Grade 4

Advanced integration of ICT, Foreign Languages, and Performance Arts.

Beyond the Classroom

Holistic Development

Athletics & Sports

Music & Arts

Our students explore their creative side through choir, traditional dance, and visual arts.

Clubs & societies

From Chess to Public Speaking, we have clubs that cater to every interest and talent.
