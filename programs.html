Our Academic Journey

18 Months – 3 Years

Early Learning Program

Our Early Learning program is a warm, play-based environment where infants and toddlers explore the world through guided discovery. Structured activities develop motor skills, socialization, and early language abilities that form the bedrock of future learning.

Play-based sensory and motor skill development
Language acquisition and social interaction
Structured daily routines for security and comfort
Qualified ECD educators on-site
Enroll for Early Learning
3 – 6 Years

ECD A & B Foundation

Our ECD A & B classes introduce structured academic content in an engaging, child-friendly way. We lay the foundations for literacy and numeracy through storytelling, creative play, music, and movement — preparing children for the transition to formal schooling.

Phonics and early reading skills
Introduction to numbers and basic mathematics
Creative arts, music, and movement activities
Social skills: sharing, listening, teamwork
Enroll for ECD
6 – 10 Years | ZIMSEC Aligned

Grade 1 to Grade 4 Primary Education

Moving into primary school, our curriculum becomes structured, following Zimbabwe National Education Standards while incorporating modern global teaching methods for 21st-century learners.

English Language

Reading comprehension, creative writing, grammar, and oral expression in an English-only environment.

  • Phonics, spelling & vocabulary
  • Oral presentations and public speaking
  • Creative writing workshops

Mathematics

Number theory, operations, geometry, and problem-solving through practical, hands-on activities.

  • Mental arithmetic & number bonds
  • Measurement & shapes
  • Real-world problem-solving

Environmental Studies

Exploration of the natural world, communities, ecosystems, and Zimbabwe's cultural heritage.

  • Nature and science experiments
  • Community and social awareness
  • Zimbabwe's heritage & culture

Social & Teamwork Skills

Group projects, conflict resolution, leadership development, and collaborative learning activities.

  • Group projects & teamwork
  • Leadership and responsibility
  • Conflict resolution skills
Beyond the Classroom

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in holistic development. Our extracurricular programs build discipline, fitness, digital skills, and teamwork in every learner.

Computer Literacy

Digital skills training from Grade 1. Students learn basic computing, typing, and internet safety in our computer lab.

Taekwondo

Certified Taekwondo coaching builds discipline, self-defense awareness, physical fitness, and mental focus.

Swimming

Swimming lessons develop water confidence, a vital life skill. Classes are supervised by trained instructors.

Tennis

Tennis coaching introduces coordination, agility, and a love of sport through fun, age-appropriate training sessions.

National Standards

ZIMSEC-Aligned Curriculum

Our full curriculum is aligned with the Zimbabwe School Examinations Council (ZIMSEC) framework, ensuring every learner at Alpha Junior School is fully prepared for Zimbabwe's national educational standards and future examinations.

Beyond ZIMSEC requirements, we supplement with international best practices in early childhood education, integrating modern teaching methodologies that develop critical thinking and creativity from an early age.
