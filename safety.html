Campus Hygiene

Sanitation & Facilities

We maintain the highest cleanliness standards across all learning spaces. Our dedicated Hygiene and Safety Instructor oversees daily sanitation, proper waste disposal, and ensures all students follow handwashing routines after meals and outdoor activities.

Age-Appropriate Toilets

Specially designed facilities for ECD learners to maintain independence and hygiene.

Borehole Water Supply

Clean, reliable borehole water ensures safe drinking water and hygiene facilities year-round.

Safe Waste Disposal

Regular waste collection and safe disposal procedures maintained across the entire campus.

Reliable Shuttles

Safe Door-to-Door Transport

We provide daily door-to-door transport for students living across Chivhu and nearby areas. Our fully licensed, vetted drivers adhere to the highest road safety standards — ensuring every child arrives and returns home safely and on time.

Routes are carefully planned to minimize travel time while maximizing coverage. Each vehicle accommodates a responsible adult attendant for young learners.
